Tourism Award Application Prompt

I'm preparing an application for a tourism industry award in [category/name of award]. My business focuses on [brief description of your tourism business]. Key aspects I want to highlight include: - Our cultural integrity and sustainability practices - Our visitor experience and feedback (with [X]% satisfaction rate) - Our community impact and engagement - Our innovation in [specific area] Please help me structure my award application with: 1. An outline of key sections to include 2. Suggestions for how to present our data effectively 3. Tips for making our cultural values and connection to place stand out 4. A simple system to collect and organize supporting evidence Please keep suggestions practical for a small team with limited time.

Visitor Experience Enhancement Prompt

I run a [type] tourism experience focused on sharing [cultural/natural] aspects of [location/region]. We want to enhance our visitor journey while maintaining authenticity. Our current visitor experience includes: - [Brief description of current experience] - Approximately [X] visitors per [day/week/month] - [X]% international visitors and [X]% domestic visitors Please suggest: 1. Three simple ways to enhance our visitor experience that respect cultural integrity 2. How we might better communicate our cultural values to visitors before they arrive 3. A system for gathering more meaningful visitor feedback that goes beyond satisfaction ratings Please focus on solutions that don't require major technology investments.

Tips for Getting the Best Results:

  1. Be specific - Edit the prompts to include details about your specific tourism operation
  2. Ask follow-up questions - If the AI's response needs clarification, ask it to explain or elaborate
  3. Request examples - Ask for concrete examples that relate to your specific context
  4. Iterate - Use the AI's response as a starting point, then refine with additional prompts
  5. Maintain cultural oversight - Always review AI suggestions through your cultural lens
